# StadiumPulse AI: Demo Walkthrough Script (3-5 Minutes)

1. **[Landing Page]** "Welcome to StadiumPulse AI. We are addressing all 8 pillars of the World Cup 2026 challenge through two distinct, AI-powered portals. Let's start with the Fan Experience."
2. **[Click Fan Portal]** "Here on the Fan Portal, accessibility is front and center. I can immediately toggle High Contrast or Large Text, and there's a dedicated button to request mobility assistance right from my seat."
3. **[Wayfinding Widget]** "For navigation, instead of a confusing map, I use the AI Wayfinding Assistant. I'll type 'How do I get to Gate C from Section 120?' and Gemini gives me step-by-step directions."
4. **[Crowd Map Widget]** "We also tackle crowd management. This live map shows congestion. Notice how the AI flags high-traffic zones and suggests alternative routes in real-time."
5. **[Transit Widget]** "As the match nears the end, the Transit ETA assistant uses AI to tell me exactly when to leave my seat to beat the rush for the NJ Transit train."
6. **[Sustainability Widget]** "We encourage sustainability by showing live stadium eco-stats and offering AI-generated, personalized eco tips for fans."
7. **[Chatbot Widget]** "Finally, for multilingual assistance, our AI Concierge auto-detects language. I'll type 'Dónde está la comida?' and it replies instantly in Spanish."
8. **[Return Home -> Click Ops Command]** "Now let's switch to the Ops Command Center, designed for staff and volunteers."
9. **[Operational Feed Widget]** "This is our operational intelligence hub. Instead of reading logs, staff can click 'Generate Shift Briefing' and Gemini synthesizes the latest data into a clear priority list for the next hour."
10. **[Incident Advisor Widget]** "And for real-time decision support, we have the AI Incident Advisor. If a volunteer finds a suspicious bag, they type it in here, and Gemini instantly returns a prioritized action checklist and an escalation recommendation."
11. **[Closing]** "From navigation to incident response, StadiumPulse AI proves how generative AI can transform the 2026 World Cup experience for both fans and staff."
